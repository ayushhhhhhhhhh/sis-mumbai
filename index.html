More about SIS

With the finest educators following a unique international curriculum and a sprawling campus with state-of-the-art infrastructure, SIS offers an incomparable educational experience. With only a select group of students cherry - picked each year, we ensure every child gets undivided attention and thrives to the fullest.

Here, Carpe Diem (Seize the Day) isn't just a phrase, it's a choice students make every day. By inspiring them to excel, both in academics and extracurricular activities, we ensure they're 'Ready for the World. Ready for the Future.' Learning and living with peers from over 26 nationalities offers exposure to different cultures, broadens horizons and prepares students for excellence on a global stage. Every student is unique. And at SIS, he's celebrated and motivated to follow his passions, so he can leave his own inimitable mark on the world.
